This is just a short summary of some of my favorite projects that I am working on or have worked on. Most of what I work on is open-source and MIT licensed which means you can do pretty much anything you want. I would love feedback either via email or issues on the project’s respective issue tracker.

Personal Projects

  • dotfiles - My semi-curated collection of dotfiles.
  • blog - The content and generator for this site.
  • feltnerm-pelican-theme - The theme for this site.
  • docfu - A custom-made static site generator that creates the Fine Uploader documentation.
  • … and more.

Contributed to …

  • ajax-form - Polymer/WebComponents library for working with the <form> element.

Fine Uploader

  • fine-uploader - Client-side upload library.
  • fine-uploader-server - An express server that handles Fine Uploader requests for traditional and S3 storage backends.

Widen Media Collective

  • media-collective - A node/browser javascript client to Widen Media Collective’s REST API.
  • collective-instant - Client-side searching of Collective and display of results in real time.
  • colletive-oauth-server - A dead simple OAuth2 server that integrates with Collective.
  • collective-upload - A simple, proof-of-concept of client-side uploading to Collective.
  • widenbot - A bot for our Slack channels.

Smartimage

  • smartimage - A node(/browser) javascript client to Smartimage’s REST API.
